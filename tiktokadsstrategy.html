03 ‚Äî Strategic Approach

Two-Phase Launch Strategy

We recommend a phased approach: start with website conversion campaigns to build pixel data and prove creative, then integrate TikTok Shop for native commerce.

Phase 1: Website Conversions Weeks 1-6

  • Standard In-Feed Ads + Spark Ads driving to shopteddyrose.com
  • Build TikTok pixel data for audience optimization
  • Test creative formats and hooks with existing checkout flow
  • Create custom and lookalike audiences from converters
  • Lower operational complexity‚Äîorders flow through existing Shopify
  • Prove ROAS before adding TikTok Shop complexity

Phase 2: TikTok Shop + GMV Max Weeks 7+

  • Open TikTok Seller Center account and sync Shopify catalog
  • Enable in-app checkout for frictionless purchases
  • Launch GMV Max campaigns with proven creative
  • Activate affiliate program to expand creator reach
  • Test Video Shopping Ads with product tags
  • Explore LIVE Shopping for weekly arrivals launches

Why Phase Website First?

TikTok Shop adds operational complexity: separate order management, shipping logistics, and commission structures. Starting with website campaigns lets us prove the channel works before adding that complexity. It also builds pixel data that improves Phase 2 targeting. If Phase 1 hits 2.5X+ ROAS, we have strong signal to invest in Shop infrastructure.

07 ‚Äî TikTok Shop Setup (Phase 2)

Seller Center Onboarding

TikTok Shop setup typically takes 3-5 days for account validation. Here's the step-by-step process to prepare for Phase 2.

Step 1
Register Seller Center Account
Go to seller-us.tiktok.com. Register with business email. Upload required documents: business license, tax ID, bank account details.
Step 2
Connect Shopify Integration
Install TikTok app in Shopify. Authorize catalog sync. Products, inventory, and pricing automatically sync to TikTok Shop.
Step 3
Configure Shipping & Returns
Set shipping templates (standard, expedited). Define return policy. TikTok requires clear policies for Shop approval.
Step 4
Link TikTok Business Account
Connect Teddy & Rose TikTok account to Seller Center. This enables Spark Ads, product tagging in organic posts, and LIVE Shopping.
Step 5
Enable Shop Ads
Connect Ad Account to Seller Center. Launch GMV Max campaigns. Set minimum ROAS target and daily budget.
